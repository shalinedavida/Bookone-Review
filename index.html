Memoir of A Mixed

The book outlines the struggles of Noah Trevor as a mixed person
born during apartheid in SouthAfrica and how life was for him during
and after that time and how he is clearly the odd ball almost
everywhere he goes and the racism and segregation he encountered as a mixed
person born to a white swiss father and black mother.
